How to Contribute to Mrrp!

Mrrp! is a community-run, free and open-source software (FOSS) platform built and maintained by volunteers. Your contributions keep the project independent and sustainable. Here are the primary ways you can get involved:

1. Software & Code Contributions

Our services and platform configuration are managed transparently. You can help by reviewing code, fixing layout bugs, or writing configurations for new FOSS packages.

2. Hosting & Infrastructure

If you have spare server capacity or bandwidth, you can join our decentralized hosting cluster or run a mirror instance of one of our FOSS services.

3. Community Moderation & Support

Help new community members onboard, write support guides, or volunteer as a moderator in our chat channels to keep Mrrp! a safe and welcoming space.

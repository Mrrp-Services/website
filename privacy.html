Privacy Policy

Your privacy is a fundamental value of Mrrp! Space. This Privacy Policy details the minimal data we collect and how we process it.

1. Data Collection & Usage

We collect only the absolute minimum data required to run and secure our services:

  • Session Cookies: We use temporary session cookies strictly for authentication and service functionality. We do not use advertising, tracking, or third-party cookies.
  • Server & Application Logs: Temporary server logs (including IP addresses, browser types, and request times) are collected solely to prevent abuse, secure the network, and troubleshoot performance. These logs are automatically rotated and deleted.
  • Account Information: If you register an account on any hosted service, we store only the credentials (username/email and hashed passwords) necessary to provide access.

2. No Sharing or Sale of Data

We never sell, rent, monetize, or share your data with advertisers, third-party analytics trackers, or commercial entities. All data stays under our direct control.

3. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your session data or registered accounts. Because we do not log or track personal data beyond service operation, database deletion of your account removes all of your persistent information.

4. Data Security

We employ standard server hardening, encryption, and network security measures to safeguard the platform and any stored accounts from unauthorized access or breaches.

5. Privacy Contact

If you have questions about this policy or wish to request data deletion, please visit our Support Page.
